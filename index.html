The Movie Page

Though I am a computer science student, one of my greatest passions has been filmmaking. I love the art of film but more importantly I love to just watch movies. Film is still a very young art form, yet it has spread across the world and into nearly everyone's homes. People love to go to the movies, talk about what they have recently seen, and most common of all, criticize movies. They have become an inescapable part of our culture. From indie art films to million dollar studio blockbusters like Iron Man. This page will delve into the history of film and its contempory status.
